Basic First Aid Tips

  • CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation): Place hands in the center of the chest and give 30 compressions followed by 2 breaths.
  • Choking: Perform the Heimlich maneuver to dislodge the object.
  • Burns: Cool the burn under cold running water for 10 minutes, then cover with a sterile dressing.
  • Bleeding: Apply pressure to the wound using a clean cloth to stop the bleeding.
